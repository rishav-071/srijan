ABOUT US

"FATS," the Drama Society of IIIT Bhubaneswar, is a vibrant and creative community that serves as the heartbeat of the institute's performing arts scene. FATS, an acronym for "Fine Arts and Theatrical Society," is dedicated to cultivating a passion for drama, theater, and the arts among the students of IIIT Bhubaneswar.
