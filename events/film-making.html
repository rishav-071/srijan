50hr Film Making

A 50-hour film-making competition is an exhilarating and intense event where participants have a mere day to conceive, shoot, edit, and present a short film. The challenge not only tests filmmakers' technical skills but also demands quick thinking, creativity, and teamwork. It fosters a unique and intense environment, pushing filmmakers to think creatively, work collaboratively, and deliver remarkable results within a tight timeframe.
