is not just an event.

Srijan mai hai Entertainment Entertainment aur bharpur Entertainment.

College mai bore lag raha hai😫😫 aur Padh padh ke thaq gae ho😩, koi baat nahi FATS hai na. To kursee ki peti bandhlo kyun ki Apke samne pesh karte hai SRIJAN 2k24😀😀🥳🥳🥳. Srijan mai apke liye pesh hai kai tarah ke majedar events. Dramebaz ho ya phir chutakula sunana pasand hai ya phir jhoothi kahaniyan banani ati hai, agar kuch bhi notanki karna ata hai to Srijan tumhare liye hi hai. Kuch nahi ata to bhi participate karo kya pata kuch acha hi sikh jao, agar kuch nahi karna to dekhne hi ajao aur dusro ka hosla badhao, agli baar participate kar lena. Itna hi nahi ek raaz ki baat bata raha hun kisiko mat batana🤫🤫🤐 Winner aur runner up ke liye prizes bhi hai🥳🥳🥳. Aur haan yaad rahe ye sab free hai, log free ki chai nahi chorte ye to phir bhi entetainment hai.

Weekends mai bahar to sab jate hai, par kyun na bahar wali masti college ke andar kare. To iss weekend pe koi dusra plan nahi aur agar kuch banaya hai to cancle kardo aur ajao SRIJAN 2k24 mai. Aur janna chahte ho to niche scroll karo⬇️🔽
